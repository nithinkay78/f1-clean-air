Why F1 Clean Air

In Formula 1, "clean air" is the aerodynamic advantage a car gains when running free from the turbulent wake of the car ahead — faster, more stable, more predictable. Every other analytics tool gives you the noisy, dirty-air view: raw numbers, cluttered dashboards, data without meaning. F1 Clean Air strips that away. It is not a live timing display — it's an intelligent second screen that tells you what is about to happen.
